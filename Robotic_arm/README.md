# ROBOTIC ARM

***INITIAL POSITION 90 DEGREES***
 
***SERVO 1-***
     
    PRESS 1 FOR INCREMENTING BY 20 DEGREES
     
    PRESS 2 FOR DECREMENTING BY 20 DEGREES
    
***SERVO 2-***
    
     PRESS 3 FOR INCREMENTING BY 20 DEGREES
    
     PRESS 4 FOR DECREMENTING BY 20 DEGREES
    
***SERVO 3-***
    
     PRESS 5 FOR INCREMENTING BY 20 DEGREES*
  
     PRESS 6 FOR DECREMENTING BY 20 DEGREES* 

**REQUIRED COMPONENTS-**

     1. Arduino UNO
 
     2. Software- Arduino IDE
 
     3. jumper wires (male-male,male-female)
 
     4. Servo motors(x3)
 
     5. breadboard
